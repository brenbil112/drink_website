About Highballer

Highballer is a cocktail discovery and recipe platform designed for anyone who enjoys great drinks — from classic cocktails to modern creations.

Our goal is to make it easy to browse, view, and mix cocktail recipes, as well as create shopping lists for entertaining or personal use.

Highballer is built as a simple, user-friendly app for discovering drinks worth sharing, whether you're a home bartender or just exploring new ideas.
